Synopsis

Claris, a young and ambitious knight, begins experiencing vivid dreams—visions of a past life where she stood on the brink of death. In each vision, she was mysteriously saved by a girl named Miria.

As the dreams intensify, Claris slowly uncovers the truth: Miria lives within her, a guardian spirit bound to her soul, awakening only when Claris’s life hangs in the balance. With Miria’s hidden power, Claris battles demons, roaming beasts, and dark forces that threaten the realm.

On her journey, Claris forms deep friendships and earns the respect of her master. But when they witness Miria’s powers firsthand, they realize a dangerous secret— Powerful demons are hunting Claris, driven by their desire to claim Miria for themselves.

What lies behind Miria’s true identity? What past connects the two girls so deeply? As forgotten memories surface and ancient enemies rise, Claris must confront the truth of who she once was… and what she must become.

  • Upgradation

  • Important Info

  • Every character can only equip his/her preferred weapon
  • Iron/Void, Cobalt/Gale Equipments are crafted, upgraded and enhanced by Jessie
  • Mythril/Glacial Equipments are crafted, upgraded and enhanced by Juno
  • Obsidian/Inferno Equipments are crafted, upgraded and enhanced by Dogo
  • To upgrade an equipment from metal to Soul Element, you first need to upgrade it thrice
  • Ecnite is used to enhance equipments

Materials

  • Bones

  • Hides

  • Mana

  • Important Info

  • Materials can be obtained by slaying beasts/demons or from chests
  • Weaker materials can be exchanged to stronger ones with the value of 1/4
  • Tier 1 -> 2 Materials can be exchanged from the material trader at Southern District
  • Tier 2 -> 3 Materials can be exchanged from the material trader at Ademn Village
  • Tier 3 -> 4 Materials can be exchanged from the material trader at Hargash Port
  • Tier 1, 2, 3 Materials and boss drops can be obtained from material Trader at Southern District by Material Coupons
