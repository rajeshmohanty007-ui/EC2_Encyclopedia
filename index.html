Synopsis

Claris, a young and ambitious knight, begins experiencing vivid dreams—visions of a past life where she stood on the brink of death. In each vision, she was mysteriously saved by a girl named Miria.

As the dreams intensify, Claris slowly uncovers the truth: Miria lives within her, a guardian spirit bound to her soul, awakening only when Claris’s life hangs in the balance. With Miria’s hidden power, Claris battles demons, roaming beasts, and dark forces that threaten the realm.

On her journey, Claris forms deep friendships and earns the respect of her master. But when they witness Miria’s powers firsthand, they realize a dangerous secret— Powerful demons are hunting Claris, driven by their desire to claim Miria for themselves.

What lies behind Miria’s true identity? What past connects the two girls so deeply? As forgotten memories surface and ancient enemies rise, Claris must confront the truth of who she once was… and what she must become.
